Sports

Sport encompasses organized physical contests, ranging from global team sports like football and cricket to individual tennis matches,driven by competition, skill, and leisure. It serves as a vital cultural element and, according to sportanddev.org, a tool for development, fostering community, health, and peace.

Type's of Sports:

  • Cricket

  • Hockey

    • Hockey is a family of stick-and-ball or stick-and-puck sports in which two teams compete to drive a ball or disk into the opponent's goal. Key types include ice hockey, field hockey, roller hockey, floorball, and para ice hockey, with variants like underwater hockey and broomball adding diversity.
